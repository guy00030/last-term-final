Jewellery

Cas Greenman

Local designer and owner of Vanilla. All jewellery is made in Ottawa/Gatineau using semi-precious stones, fresh water pearls, crystals, silvers and gold plated pewter. A new addition are our vintage African brass and trading beads

White Metal from Turkey

Traditional and modern designs made in a zinc based white metal that is 98% hypoallergenic, contains no nickel and doesn't tarnish.

Claudine Moncion

Ottawa/Gatineau artisan. All designs are made from found beach stones and other objects. Each piece is wrapped in silver plated wire to create one of a kind pendants and earings.

Kristin Ross

Canadian designer from Niagra Falls, Ontario. Each unique, one of a kind piece is created using antique buttons, wood, shell, stone or crystal. All of the findings are either antique bronze, silver plated or sterling silver.

Cheryl Jacobs

Jewellery designer and silversmith in Denman Island, BC. Her distinct style of silver jewellery includes earrings, bracelets, and pendants. Cheryl Jacobs is also recognized for her custom gold jewellery or custom silver jewellery pieces. Her pearl and gemstone jewellry pieces are her most popular sellers.
